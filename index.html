Welcome :)

GeoBlacklight is a multi-institutional open-source collaboration building a better way to find and share geospatial data.

The GeoBlacklight Ruby on Rails Engine is the primary software project. This application allows you to deploy a discovery application for your own and other institution's geospatial data.
