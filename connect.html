Connect with the GeoBlacklight Community

GeoBlacklight is more than just software, it's about the community. There are lots of different ways to connect with the community and get involved with the project.

  • Join the Google Group

    For feature announcments, discussion about the project, technical questions, the GeoBlacklight Google Group is a great way to get connected.

  • Chat on Slack

    GeoBlacklight has a Slack channel. Sign up for the Slack channel to instantly talk to others in the community.

  • Follow GeoBlacklight on Twitter

    You can follow the latest news on the project on Twitter @GeoBlacklight.

  • Monthly Community Meeting

    The GeoBlacklight Community Meeting takes place on the second Wednesday of every month. Take a look at the meeting agenda and join in on Google Meet.

  • Discuss on GitHub

    Do you have an issue you want to report? Or maybe there is a piece of documentation missing. You can create an issue on GeoBlacklight's GitHub issue tracker.

  • Free your metadata

    GeoBlacklight gets even better when organizations share their metadata. Start contributing your metadata to OpenGeoMetadata or use metadata on OpenGeoMetadata for your GeoBlacklight installation.
